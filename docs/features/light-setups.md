# Light Setups & HDRIs

The **Render Settings** tab gives you full control over the lighting environment used when rendering asset previews.

---

## HDRI Environments

9 HDRI environments are bundled with the addon:

| Name | Character |
|---|---|
| City | Urban, hard shadows |
| Courtyard | Neutral, outdoor |
| Forest | Natural, soft green tones |
| Interior | Warm indoor light |
| Night | Dark, moody |
| Studio | Clean, professional |
| Sunrise | Warm golden hour |
| Sunset | Warm orange tones |

Select an HDRI from the dropdown — a live preview icon is shown for each option.

You can also adjust:
- **Exposure** — Brighten or darken the environment
- **Color Temperature** — Shift the HDRI towards warm or cool tones

---

## Light Setups

5 preset light configurations are available, shown with thumbnail previews:

- **Cold 3-Point** — Cool toned three-point lighting
- **Warm 3-Point** — Warm toned three-point lighting
- **Retro 3-Point** — Stylized retro look

---

## Backdrop & Floor

### Backdrop
- **Color** — Base color of the background plane
- **Strength** — Emissive brightness of the backdrop

### Floor
- **Color** — Base color of the floor material
- **Metallic** — Metallic value (0 = matte, 1 = fully metallic)
- **Roughness** — Surface roughness (0 = mirror, 1 = fully rough)

### Transparent Background
Enable this to render with an alpha channel — useful if you want to place the asset preview on a custom background.
