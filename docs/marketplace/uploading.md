# Uploading Assets

> **Coming soon** — Asset uploading is currently in development.

---

## How It Will Work

1. Mark your asset and render a preview thumbnail (see [Rendering Previews](../features/render-previews.md))
2. In the **Asset Manager panel**, click **Upload to PolyAssetVault**
3. Fill in the upload details:
   - Title and description
   - Category and tags
   - Price
4. Click **Submit** — the asset is packaged and uploaded to the marketplace

---

> You must be [logged in](login.md) to upload assets.
