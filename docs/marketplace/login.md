> **Coming soon** — Marketplace login is currently in development.

---

## How It Will Work

1. Click **Login to PolyAssetVault** in the BullTools panel
2. Your browser opens the PolyAssetVault login page
3. Sign in with your account credentials
4. Blender receives the authentication token automatically
5. Your profile and purchased assets appear in the addon

No password is stored in Blender — authentication uses a secure token flow.

---

## Your Profile

Once logged in, the addon will display:
- Your username and avatar
- Your purchased assets (with previews)
- Your uploaded assets
